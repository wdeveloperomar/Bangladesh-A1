Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Embracing technology and creativity to drive growth, efficiency, and global competitiveness.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly practices for long-term economic, environmental, and social stability.

Infrastructure

Developing robust systems to support connectivity, trade, and modernization across all sectors.

Empowerment

Uplifting communities through education, skill development, and equitable access to opportunities.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

"Today, we stand at a crossroads in our nation's journey. Together, we must embrace innovation, sustainability, and empowerment to create a prosperous future. Our people are the greatest asset of Bangladesh, and empowering them through education, entrepreneurship, and equitable opportunities will unlock their limitless potential. Let us build a nation where every individual contributes to progress, free from poverty and inequality. By focusing on sustainable growth and strong infrastructure, we can secure a brighter tomorrow for generations to come. Join me in working towards a unified vision of a better Bangladesh—one built on hope, dignity, and shared success.

Thank you."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
